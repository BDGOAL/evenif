III. Critical Insights & Root Cause Analysis

✅ Key Highlights

1. General Stabilization 🏆

Revenue +18.8%, Orders +15.4%, Traffic +18.9%. New customer acquisition grew significantly by +55.6%.

2. Social Media Rebound 🚀

Traffic +50.2% with CVR jumping to 0.88%. Content strategy or ad quality has improved significantly.

3. Checkout Flow Efficiency 🚀

Checkout success rate at 83.3%. High-intent users are successfully navigating to completion.

4. Hero Product Success 🚀

Alcohol-free sparkling wine alone contributed 42.2% of revenue. Scale stock and promotion accordingly.

🚨 Core Issues Diagnosis

Issue 1: Drastic Drop in ATC Rate (-40%)

Data: Rate dropped from 5.75% to 3.45%

Causes: Traffic mix shift (social users have lower intent), lack of PDP urgency (scarcity, reviews), new SKUs lack social proof.

ACTION: Scarcity prompts, enhance UGC modules, ATC popup discounts.

Issue 2: Direct Traffic Decline (-23%)

Causes: Decline in brand loyalty/return visits, EDM reach issues, drop in repeat customers (only 1 this period).

ACTION: Increase EDM frequency, launch point system, automated 30-day reminders.

Issue 3: Retention Rate Volatility

Data: 6.3% vs 13.3%

ACTION: Post-purchase care flows, exclusive loyalty discounts, refill notifications.
